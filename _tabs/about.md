Hi, I’m Andrew Filewich, a kinesiology student at the University of Calgary with prior frontline nursing experience and a growing interest in research, rehabilitation, kinesiology education, and applied health practice.

This website serves as my professional and academic portfolio. It brings together several parts of my work, including my resume, my honours thesis, and a co-authored publication on the impact of COVID-19 restrictions on family time in long-term care.

## Academic and Professional Interests

My interests include:

- kinesiology education and experiential learning
- multimedia instruction and educational design
- exercise physiology and performance testing
- rehabilitation and applied movement science
- qualitative health research
- family-centered care and health policy

## Current Featured Project

The central project on this site is my honours thesis:

**Pre-Laboratory Instructional Videos in Experiential Learning: A Kinesiology Perspective**

This project examined whether short pre-laboratory instructional videos could help undergraduate kinesiology students feel more prepared, more confident, and more clear on laboratory procedures before entering lab sessions.

## What This Site Includes

This portfolio includes:

- a live resume page outlining my education, experience, and skills
- a web-based presentation of my honours thesis
- a co-authored publication on long-term care, COVID-19 restrictions, and family involvement
- programmatic data visuals based on thesis findings
- selected academic and professional materials that reflect my broader development

## Purpose of This Portfolio

I created this site to present my work in a format that is more accessible, organized, and practical than a traditional document alone. It is intended to showcase both my academic development and my broader professional interests in health, research, rehabilitation, and education.
